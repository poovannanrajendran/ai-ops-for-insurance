# Placement Tracker Sample Sources

All sample files in this directory are synthetic and were created for Day 18 validation only.

## Files
- `smart-follow-gap.csv`: open-market bottleneck sample with stale follow-ups and reduced capacity.
- `steady-placed-book.csv`: balanced placement sample with mostly signed capacity and limited open tail.
- `required-field-gate.csv`: intentionally incomplete extract for required-field validation testing.

## Notes
- No production broker, market, or insured data is included.
- Shares, premiums, and line sizes are deterministic fixtures chosen to exercise the scoring and route logic.
