# Sources

All three samples in this folder are synthetic and were authored for Day 15 of the project.

They reflect common London-market broker submission patterns:
- labelled broker email or renewal-summary fields
- property submission with complete controls and clean claims history
- product liability submission with North American exposure, recall activity, and large-line referral triggers
- intentionally incomplete note for required-field gate testing

No third-party proprietary submission data is included.
