# D13 - Day 13: Schedule overlap should be flagged before accumulation surprises emerge

Two schedules can look independent while carrying the same risk concentration.
Day 13 detects cross-schedule clashes and prioritises them by severity.

Problem: Overlaps across policy schedules are hard to spot manually at portfolio scale.

MVP achieved: Clash detector matches location/peril/period collisions and produces ranked clash alerts with persistence and audit trails.

Scale path: Network graph views, clash trend tracking, and automated referral orchestration across underwriting teams.

CTA: Which clash dimension matters most for you: location, peril, period, or all three?
