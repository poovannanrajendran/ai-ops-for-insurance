# D25 - Day 25: Sanctions checks should be repeatable, explainable, and schedule-driven

Screening quality drops when list updates and checks are disconnected.
Day 25 screens entities deterministically and captures clear block/review outcomes.

Problem: Manual screening is inconsistent and hard to evidence during assurance reviews.

MVP achieved: Screening aid evaluates parties against sanctions references, returns disposition + rationale, and logs audit trails.

Scale path: Automated list refresh jobs, fuzzy-match tuning, and workflow handoff to compliance operations.
