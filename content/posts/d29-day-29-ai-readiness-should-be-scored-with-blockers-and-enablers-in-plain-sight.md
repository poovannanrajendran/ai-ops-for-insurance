# D29 - Day 29: AI readiness should be scored with blockers and enablers in plain sight

Readiness discussions stall when maturity is subjective and unstructured.
Day 29 scores readiness, surfaces blockers, and proposes a practical execution roadmap.

Problem: Organisations struggle to align capability, governance, and execution steps into one readiness view.

MVP achieved: Scorer evaluates maturity dimensions, classifies band/confidence, and returns a ninety-day action plan with persistence.

Scale path: Periodic re-scoring, initiative tracking, and executive portfolio governance views.

CTA: What is your biggest readiness blocker today: data, governance, or operating model?
