# D23 - Day 23: Operational health should be monitored as risk, not vanity metrics

Ops metrics matter only when they trigger clear intervention.
Day 23 turns service telemetry into a risk-rated operations action plan.

Problem: Teams track many KPIs, but escalation decisions are inconsistent and delayed.

MVP achieved: Health monitor scores ops indicators, categorises risk levels, and generates focused remediation actions with auditability.

Scale path: Real-time monitoring feeds, trend thresholds, and leadership-ready control dashboards.

CTA: Which KPI should trigger immediate intervention in your operating model?
