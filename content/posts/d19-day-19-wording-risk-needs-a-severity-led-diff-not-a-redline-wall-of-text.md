# D19 - Day 19: Wording risk needs a severity-led diff, not a redline wall of text

Teams do not need every wording change. They need materiality-first decisions.
Day 19 scores wording deltas by risk severity and highlights actionable differences.

Problem: Traditional diff reviews create noise and bury material changes in volume.

MVP achieved: Risk diff checker classifies wording changes by severity, surfaces critical snippets, and persists analysis plus audit trail.

Scale path: Clause ontology, historical precedent matching, and automatic routing for legal/underwriting review.

CTA: If you had one severity threshold for auto-escalation, where would you set it?
