# D20 - Day 20: Regulatory updates should become ranked actions, not unread bulletins

Regulatory volume is high, but decisions still need to be made by deadline.
Day 20 converts multi-source updates into a relevance-ranked action digest.

Problem: Regulatory feeds are spread across sources, making prioritisation and ownership inconsistent.

MVP achieved: Digest builder parses updates, scores relevance by class focus, and outputs governance-ready actions with persistence and audit.

Scale path: Automated source ingestion, role-based routing, control mapping, and deadline tracking across compliance teams.

CTA: Which source causes the most review friction in your team today?
