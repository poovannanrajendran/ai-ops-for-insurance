# D02 - Day 2: Portfolio concentration risk should be visible before it becomes a loss

Good portfolios fail quietly when concentration builds in the wrong place.
Day 2 makes mix, territory, and limit pressure visible before the next bind.

Problem: Static spreadsheets hide accumulation and class/territory skew until someone spots the pattern late.

MVP achieved: CSV workflow that analyses mix, flags concentration warnings, and adds plain-English commentary.

Scale path: Live monitoring, scenario overlays, threshold alerts, steering dashboards, syndicate governance reporting.

CTA: Would you use this as daily monitoring or pre-bind decision aid?
