# D14 - Day 14: Leakage signals should be surfaced before reserve drift becomes expensive

Claims leakage usually starts with weak early signals, not a single obvious error.
Day 14 scores claims rows for leakage pressure and governance follow-up.

Problem: Late notifications, duplicate patterns, and reserve/paid drift are buried in large claims ledgers.

MVP achieved: Leakage flagger calculates deterministic risk scores, highlights drivers, and outputs reviewer-ready actions with audit logging.

Scale path: SIU integrations, workflow routing, recovery tracking, and continuous leakage surveillance.
