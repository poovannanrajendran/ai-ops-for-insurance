# D17 - Day 17: Market reform contract checks should be machine-speed and audit-friendly

MRC compliance reviews are high-value judgement work buried under repetitive checks.
Day 17 validates required fields and clause signals with deterministic outputs.

Problem: Manual MRC checks are time-consuming and inconsistent across reviewers.

MVP achieved: MRC checker runs required-field gates, clause checks, warnings, and full whitespace extraction status with persisted audit.

Scale path: Clause benchmark libraries, broker feedback loops, and automated exception routing to specialist reviewers.
