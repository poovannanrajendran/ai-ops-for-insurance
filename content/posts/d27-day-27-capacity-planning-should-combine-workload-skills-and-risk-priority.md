# D27 - Day 27: Capacity planning should combine workload, skills, and risk priority

Backlog management fails when capacity is treated as a single number.
Day 27 plans team allocation using demand pressure and reviewer capability signals.

Problem: Teams over-focus on queue size and under-focus on capability mix and escalation risk.

MVP achieved: Planner scores workload pressure, maps coverage gaps, and recommends action priorities with audit trail persistence.

Scale path: Shift forecasting, dynamic skill matrices, and queue-to-capacity automation loops.

CTA: Where is your largest capacity mismatch: volume, skill, or ownership?
