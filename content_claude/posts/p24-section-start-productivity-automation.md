# P24 — Section Start: Productivity — Remove Friction, Not Judgment

Every insurance team I have worked with has the same problem disguised as ten different symptoms.

"We spend too long on renewals." "The data quality is poor." "Meeting prep takes hours." "Nobody reads the regulatory updates." "The QBR narrative takes a week to write."

These are not ten problems. They are one problem: too much manual work between receiving information and acting on it.

Productivity tools in insurance are not about dashboards or analytics. They are about removing the low-value repetitive work that sits between a professional and the judgment call they are paid to make.

In Week 4, I am building tools that target the friction, not the decisions:

- **Renewal Intelligence Copilot** — Upload policy summary and claims history, get a structured renewal strategy memo
- **Ops Health Monitor** — Define KPIs, upload data, get trend analysis and anomaly detection
- **Data Quality Validator** — Upload any insurance data file, get schema validation and anomaly flagging
- **QBR Narrative Generator** — Input key metrics, get a professional QBR narrative paragraph
- **Meeting Prep Briefing** — Enter client or market name, get a structured briefing pack

The common thread: structured input → intelligent processing → professional output. The human owns the decision. The tool owns the preparation.
