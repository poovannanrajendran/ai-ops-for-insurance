# P04 — The Roadmap: 30 Apps Grouped by Insurance Workflow

I did not pick 30 random AI use cases.

I mapped every app to a specific insurance workflow, weighted by where I have seen the most operational friction across nearly two decades in the London Market.

Here is the breakdown:

**Week 1: Underwriting Core (Days 1-7)**
"I understand the heart of Lloyd's — risk selection, appetite, and portfolio management."

Submission triage. Portfolio concentration dashboard. Risk appetite parser. Slip reviewer. Class-of-business classifier. Exposure accumulation heatmap. Cat event briefing.

This is the highest-signal week. Underwriting is where most specialty insurers live, and it is where AI can deliver the fastest tangible value.

**Week 2: Claims & Exposure Deep Dive (Days 8-14)**
"I apply AI across the full policy lifecycle — not just front-office."

Endorsement diff checker. Referral priority scorer. FNOL triage assistant. Claims reserve advisor. Treaty structure explainer. Exposure clash detector. Claims leakage flagger.

Claims operations shape trust at the moment it matters most. These tools move beyond intake and into the operational backbone.

**Week 3: Broking, Placement & Market (Days 15-21)**
Broker submission builder. Exposure scenario modeller. Market reform contract checker. Placement status tracker. Wordings diff tool. Regulatory update digest. Meeting prep briefing.

**Week 4: Productivity, Leadership & Synthesis (Days 22-28)**
Renewal intelligence copilot. Ops health monitor. Data quality validator. Sanctions screening aid. QBR narrative generator. Team capacity planner. Stakeholder comms drafter.

**Week 5: Capstone (Days 29-30)**
AI readiness scorer. Challenge portfolio showcase.

**The priority weightings:**
- Lloyd's / Specialty Underwriting: 27% (8 days) — Highest
- Exposure Management: 13% (4 days) — High
- Claims Ops: 13% (4 days) — High
- Broking / Placement: 13% (4 days) — Medium
- Productivity & Automation: 17% (5 days) — Medium
- Leadership / Team Ops: 7% (2 days) — Lower
- General Insurance / Compliance: 10% (3 days) — Lower

This weighting reflects where I see the market spending the most time on manual work that AI can meaningfully reduce — without replacing judgment.

The roadmap was built using a three-source methodology: ChatGPT for value scoring, Claude for narrative arc, and Perplexity for market research. Then consolidated into the final sequence.

Every week tells a story. Every app solves a specific problem. Nothing is filler.
