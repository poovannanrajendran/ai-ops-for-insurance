## Batch Generation Tips

1. **Open Gemini** (gemini.google.com) and use the image generation feature
2. **Copy each prompt** directly — they are ready to paste
3. **Save files as:** `p01.png`, `p02.png`, `d01.png`, etc.
4. **If Gemini struggles with text-on-image:** generate the visual without text, then add the title/subtitle in Canva (free) or Figma
5. **Consistency matters:** the dark navy + teal palette ties the whole series together visually
6. **Generate launch day images first:** P01, P02, P03, P04, P08, D01 — these are the priority
