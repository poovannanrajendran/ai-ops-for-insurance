## LinkedIn Algorithm Tips

1. **No external links in the post body.** LinkedIn suppresses reach for posts with outbound URLs. Put links in the first comment instead.
2. **First 2 lines are everything.** They appear before "...see more". Every post in this pack has a scroll-stopping hook — do not edit the first two lines.
3. **Engage in the first hour.** Reply to every comment within 60 minutes of posting. The algorithm rewards early engagement.
4. **Pin P01 to your profile** for the duration of the challenge. It is the context post that makes every subsequent post meaningful.
5. **Tag sparingly.** Tag people only if they will genuinely engage. Empty tags reduce reach.
6. **Images boost reach by ~2x.** Use the Gemini image prompts in each post to generate a visual before publishing.
7. **Weekend posts still work** for insurance/fintech audiences. Saturday 08:15 performs well for thoughtful reads.
8. **Two posts per day maximum.** More than two and LinkedIn may throttle reach. On multi-post days, space them 3+ hours apart.
